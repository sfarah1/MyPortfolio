Are you Ok?

It's ok not to be ok. We all have our ups and downs, it's a part of what makes us human. Your feelings and emotions are not your enemy. They are not things that you must repress and ignore. They should be expressed, embraced and acknowledged. A problem shared is a problem halved. And that's where we come in. .

Who are we

We are a mental health support group based in Wales and are committed to supporting others with overcoming the challenges linked to mental health and substance misuse. Founded by Matthew Creel, the tidy butt team comprises a number of ambassadors that are driven to promote the support group. Such ambassadors include former-Welsh international rugby player, Rhys Thomas and others.

Don't suffer in silence

Now, more than ever, many of us are feeling the strain of the pandemic on our mental health. After long months of intermittent lockdowns, you may have noticed you’ve been feeling lethargic, sluggish or even lost confidence – particularly if you’ve been shielding. These are all signs that, maybe, things aren’t so great. But that’s okay, and in these times, perfectly normal. However, it’s still possible to do something about it. You wouldn’t ignore the symptoms of a physical medical problem, so why ignore the symptoms of poor mental health?.
